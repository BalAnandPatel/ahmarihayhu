Dr. Dhanesh Kumar Agrahari

M.B.B.S | M.S. (General Surgery) | M.Ch (Pediatric Surgery)
Designation: Senior Consultant (Pediatric Surgeon) | Phoenix Hospital, Allahabad.

Dr. Dhanesh Agrahari is a Senior Consultant - General Surgery, Laproscopic Surgery with a super specialised degree (M.Ch) in - Pediatric Surgery. He has more than 15 years experience in the field of Pediatric Surgery, Pediatric Urology and Pediatric Laproscopic Surgery. He has a unique mix of experience of having worked in Academic Institutions and Corporate Hospitals in his field.
He has operated of more than 10,000 children with various surgical problems which includes rare complex conditions. His area of expertise ranges from Pediatric General Surgical conditions and Laproscopic Surgery to Pediatric Thoracic Surgery and Vascular Surgery to Pediatric Urological conditions.
He has had an advanced training in CEMAST, Mumbai in the field of Pediatric Laproscopic Surgery and Neonatal Surgery. Currently, he is working with Phoenix Hospital as a Director and Promoter.
